📘 Welcome to Blockchain Decentralized storage

A secure and decentralized file-sharing platform using Blockchain and IPFS.

  • 🔐 Login / Register:
    New here? Click Register to create an account.
    Already a user? Just Login with your credentials.
  • 📁 Upload a File:
    Go to the Upload section.
    Choose your file — it will be AES-256 encrypted.
    File gets stored on IPFS via Pinata, and backed up on Filebase.
  • 🧾 Blockchain Logging:
    Each upload is recorded on a blockchain.
    Ensures immutability and audit trails.
  • 🧑‍💻 Admin Approval:
    Admin verifies and approves the download request.
    Unauthorized downloads are rejected.
  • ⬇️ Download:
    Once approved, go to the Download page.
    Enter your decryption key to retrieve your file.
  • 🔒 Security & Access:
    Only logged-in users can access uploads/downloads.
    Your files are private and encrypted — even the admin can’t read them!
